About Image & Video Tools

Welcome to the Image & Video Tools application! Created by Son Nguyen in 2024, its goal is to provide you with a comprehensive suite of tools for manipulating images and videos directly in your browser. Whether you need to resize an image, apply filters, convert file formats, generate thumbnails, or remove audio from a video, our tools make it easy to perform these tasks quickly and efficiently.

Each tool is designed with user-friendliness in mind, offering intuitive interfaces and seamless functionality. I will continuously strive to improve and expand our toolset to meet the needs of our users.

Thank you for using this application! I hope it helps you achieve your media editing goals with ease.

For any questions, feedback, or support, please feel free to contact me. Your input is valuable as I work to make this application even better.
